# Discord Announcement — AIRAC 2603 (19 March 2026)

@ Everyone 

📡 UK VFPC Update — AIRAC 2603 (19 March 2026)

The SRD data has been updated for the new AIRAC cycle and includes the results of the expansion of FRA airspace in Scotland.

⚠️ Note for this cycle: This is the first release produced by the new SRD parser software. The underlying route data is the same as published by NATS, but they have made some basic changes in how they present about a quarter of the routes.  So minimum flight levels, remarks and restrictions may differ from what you've seen before on familiar routes. It's also the parser being more thorough than it was before.

NATs have also dropped quite a few of their restrictions.  Looks like they too did a cleanup.  But they also are supplying many routes now with a minimum Flight Level of MC (minimum cruise level).  To supply actual numbers to you, I am now reading the UK AIP files that define the actual flyways and points and calculating the minimum level that the entire filed SRD route can be flown at, and replacing MC with my calculated route.

If you have questions or want to talk further, please use the "Talk-to-us" channel on the discord site.
